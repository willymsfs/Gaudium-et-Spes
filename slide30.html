Divine Origin of Marriage

• Sacred Institution: "Marriage and the family are institutions of divine origin, and are therefore sacred."
• Created by God: Established by God from the beginning of creation."
• Elevated by Christ: Raised to the dignity of a sacrament by Christ."
• Natural Institution: Corresponds to human nature and the Creator\"s design."
• Universal Value: Not merely a social convention but a reality with universal significance."
"God Himself is the author of matrimony, endowed as it is with various benefits and purposes. All of these have a very decisive bearing on the continuation of the human race, on the personal development and eternal destiny of the individual members of a family, and on the dignity, stability, peace and prosperity of the family itself and of human society as a whole."
