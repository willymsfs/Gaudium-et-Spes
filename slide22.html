The Value of Human Work

• Divine Participation: "Man is called to work, and to transform the world through his labor."
• Co-Creation: Through work, humans participate in God's ongoing creative activity."
• Personal Development: Work contributes to human fulfillment and self-realization."
• Social Dimension: Work builds community and serves the common good."
• Universal Dignity: All forms of honest work have dignity, not just intellectual or prestigious occupations."
"Human activity, to be sure, takes its significance from its relationship to man. Just as it proceeds from man, so it is ordered toward man. For when a man works he not only alters things and society, he develops himself as well. He learns much, he cultivates his resources, he goes outside of himself and beyond himself."
