Cooperation for the Common Good

• Cooperative Call: "All men are called to cooperate with one another in building up the human community, and in promoting the common good."
• Common Good Defined: "The sum total of social conditions which allow people, either as groups or as individuals, to reach their fulfillment more fully and more easily."
• Balance Required: Harmonizing individual rights with social responsibilities.
• Subsidiarity: Decisions should be made at the lowest appropriate level.
• Participation: All members of society should contribute according to their abilities.
"The common good embraces the sum of those conditions of social life by which individuals, families, and groups can achieve their own fulfillment in a relatively thorough and ready way."
