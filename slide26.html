Light to the Nations

• Prophetic Mission: "The Church is called to be a light to the nations, and to proclaim the Gospel to all men."
• Biblical Roots: Draws on Isaiah's prophecy of Israel as "a light to the nations" (Isaiah 49:6)."
• Universal Proclamation: The Gospel message is for all people, not just Christians."
• Witness in the World: The Church bears witness to Christ through both word and action."
• Transformative Presence: Called to transform culture and society through Gospel values."
"The Church, sent to all peoples of every time and place, is not bound exclusively and indissolubly to any race or nation, any particular way of life or any customary way of life recent or ancient. Faithful to her own tradition and at the same time conscious of her universal mission, she can enter into communion with the various civilizations, to their enrichment and the enrichment of the Church herself."
