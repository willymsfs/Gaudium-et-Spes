One Human Family

• Divine Will: "God has willed that all men should form one family, and should live together in peace and harmony."
• Universal Brotherhood: All humans are brothers and sisters, sharing a common origin and destiny.
• Solidarity: The bonds that unite all people in mutual responsibility.
• Peace as Goal: Living together in harmony is God's intention for humanity.
• Overcoming Division: Working to heal divisions caused by sin, selfishness, and injustice.
"One of the salient features of the modern world is the growing interdependence of men one on the other, a development promoted chiefly by modern technical advances. Nevertheless brotherly dialogue among men does not reach its perfection on the level of technical progress, but on the deeper level of interpersonal relationships."
