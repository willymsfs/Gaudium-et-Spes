Christ as the New Adam

• Christological Center: "Christ is the key to understanding the human person and human history."
• New Adam: Christ as the perfect human being and model for humanity.
• Restoration: In Christ, human nature is restored to its original dignity.
• Revelation: Christ reveals both God to humanity and humanity to itself.
• Transformation: Through Christ, humanity is transformed and elevated.
"The truth is that only in the mystery of the incarnate Word does the mystery of man take on light. For Adam, the first man, was a figure of Him Who was to come, namely Christ the Lord. Christ, the final Adam, by the revelation of the mystery of the Father and His love, fully reveals man to man himself and makes his supreme calling clear."
