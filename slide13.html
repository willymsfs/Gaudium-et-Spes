The Image of God

• Biblical Foundation: "Man has been made in the image of God" (Genesis 1:26-27).
• Dominion: Humans "given dominion over all creation" as stewards, not absolute owners.
• Divine Participation: "Called to share in God's own life and love."
• Source of Dignity: "This is the source of his dignity" - human worth derives from divine origin.
• Universal Application: All humans, without exception, bear this image.
"Therefore, this sacred synod, proclaiming the noble destiny of man and championing the Godlike seed which has been sown in him, offers to mankind the honest assistance of the Church in fostering that brotherhood of all men which corresponds to this destiny of theirs."
