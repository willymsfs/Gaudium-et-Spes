Justice and Charity in Work

• Ethical Framework: "Man's work should be carried out in a spirit of justice and charity" (Gaudium et Spes, 67)
• Just Wages: Workers have a right to compensation that provides for dignified living
• Fair Conditions: Work environments must respect human dignity and safety
• Charity Beyond Justice: While justice establishes minimum requirements, charity calls for generosity
• Structural Reform: Need for just economic structures, not merely individual good intentions
"Among the basic rights of the human person is to be numbered the right of freely founding unions for working people... Included is the right of freely taking part in the activity of these unions without risk of reprisal." (Gaudium et Spes, 68)
